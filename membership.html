Membership

Membership at Vitara Gaia Wellness House is intentionally limited and deeply personal.

Vitara Gaia Wellness House welcomes a limited number of members each season. We prioritize depth, discretion, and continuity of care over volume.

Who the House Is For

  • • Founders, creatives, and executives seeking sustainable performance.
  • • Individuals recovering from burnout, injury, or chronic imbalance.
  • • Members drawn to ritual, privacy, and integrative medicine.

Membership Privileges

  • • Access to House Rituals and Immersive Days.
  • • Priority scheduling for in-residence sessions.
  • • Ongoing integrative oversight (TCM, functional, herbal, peptide/hormone).
  • • Invitations to intimate salon evenings (tea, lectures, longevity talks).
  • • Culinary Medicine experiences during immersions or retreats.
  • • Ability to host a guest (with approval) for certain rituals.

Membership Pathway

  1. Submit a private inquiry.
  2. Short conversation to align intentions and needs.
  3. If aligned, a membership outline is extended.
  4. Initiation through a first immersion at the House.
