Membership

A Private Pact With Your Health

Membership at Vitara Gaia is an ongoing relationship — intimate, seasonal, and designed for those seeking depth rather than transactions.

Vitara Gaia Membership is not a package, not a punch card, and not a “spa plan.” It is an ongoing relationship with a private integrative wellness house that knows your story, tracks your patterns, and walks with you through each season of your life.

Membership is intentionally limited so that every person is treated as a house member, not a customer.

Clinical & Ritual Care

  • • Priority access to extended, layered sessions blending acupuncture, electro-acupuncture, cupping, gua sha, bodywork, IV therapy, ozone, peptides, skincare rituals, and nervous system work.
  • • Long-form intakes and reviews rather than rushed appointments.
  • • Care plans that shift with your seasons: stress cycles, menstruation, fertility, postpartum, injury recovery, peri-menopause, burnout, performance windows, and travel.

Apothecary & Nutritional Medicine

  • • Private herbal and tonic prescriptions, blended for your constitution.
  • • Delivery or pick-up for herbs, tinctures, tonics.
  • • Seasonal meals delivered (premade) or ingredient-based wellness boxes to prepare at home.

Access & Support

  • • Priority scheduling for sessions and ritual days.
  • • 24-hour access to medical and concierge support within agreed boundaries.
  • • Nervous system–aware support between sessions: check-ins, adjustments to herbs, ritual guidance.

Guests, Travel & Retreats

  • • Invite select guests into The House for curated rituals.
  • • Access to medical tourism and wellness retreats—local and international.
  • • Priority access as new Houses or pop-ups open worldwide.

How Membership Works

1 — Application

Submit a brief application or request an introduction so we may understand your needs and intentions.

2 — Immersion

A deep intake, extended treatment ritual, and an initial apothecary + nutritional plan.

3 — Monthly Rhythm

Regular sessions, check-ins, herbal adjustments, and seasonal recalibrations.

4 — Evolution

As your life shifts, your membership evolves—deepening your relationship with The House.

A Month Inside the House

In one month, a member might experience:

  • — A 2-hour ritual treatment blending acupuncture, bodywork, and IV therapy tailored to workload and sleep.
  • — A revised herbal formula delivered to their home for digestion, anxiety, or hormonal shifts.
  • — A pre-flight check-in adjusting herbs for jet lag and immune support.
  • — A seasonal group ritual—sound, tea, moxa—with other members.
  • — A quiet session focused on nervous system integration and recalibration.

Membership Boundaries

  • • Membership spots are limited to protect intimacy.
  • • We are out-of-network; superbills available when appropriate.
  • • 24-hour access means respectful, not constant, communication.
  • • We are a wellness house, not an emergency facility.
