A Private Integrative Wellness Sanctuary

Vitara Gaia is a concierge-style wellness and integrative medicine concept that blends Traditional Oriental Medicine, modern longevity care, and curated hospitality into a single, ritualized experience.

Rather than offering isolated treatments, Vitara Gaia designs intentional wellness environments spaces where guests move through care in a thoughtful sequence that calms the nervous system, restores the body, and nourishes the senses.

A place where modern medicine meets ancient earth… each experience is structured as a wellness journey, not a clinical appointment.

How The House Works

A visit to Vitara Gaia unfolds like a story. You cross the threshold and are met with a welcome ritual, a warm towel, a quiet greeting, a herbal infusion crafted for your current state.

Your intake is a conversation, not an interrogation. We read your pulse, your tongue, your sleep patterns, your stress cycles, your emotional rhythms.

Your treatment becomes an orchestration of ritual medicine:

  • Acupuncture or electro-acupuncture
  • Cupping, gua sha, somatic bodywork
  • Ozone, peptides, IV therapy
  • Skinwork, regenerative aesthetics
  • Breathwork, sound, and nervous-system rituals
  • Herbal infusions, teas, tinctures
  • Programs for rest, detox, clarity, renewal

Sessions do not begin and end... they unfold.
