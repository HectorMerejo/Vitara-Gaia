The House

Vitara Gaia Wellness House is a private integrative wellness residence.

Situated in New York City, Vitara Gaia Wellness House is a quiet, curated environment for those seeking longevity, detox, and rehabilitation. It is not a public clinic— it is a house, accessible only to members and their invited guests.

The House is designed with in-residence intimacy: low daily volume, extended sessions, and sensory cues crafted to settle the nervous system. Members arrive to be received, not rushed.

House Atmosphere

Penthouse stillness, townhouse warmth — soft light, stone, aroma, and textiles that hold the nervous system.
