The House

Vitara Gaia House is a private integrative wellness residence.

Situated in New York City, Vitara Gaia House is a quiet, curated environment for those seeking longevity, detox, and rehabilitation. It is not a public clinic—it is a house, accessible only to members and their invited guests.

The House is designed with in-residence intimacy: low daily volume, extended time-blocks, and deeply considered sensory cues. Members arrive to be received, not rushed. Every ritual is staged as a private experience.

House Atmosphere

Penthouse stillness, townhouse warmth. Soft light, stone, and textiles that hold the nervous system. Aromas from the culinary salon and apothecary integrate the senses into each ritual.

The House is intentionally low-traffic to preserve a sense of residence rather than rotation. At any moment, only a small number of members may be present.
